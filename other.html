Practical Course - Molecular Dynamics

In this practical course (WiSe 20/21) we worked in a team of three in order to develop a framework in C++ for simulating gases and liquids at a molecular level. Molecule clusters were also used to create solid bodies. Although the code could not be made public, I attached some of the simulations below.

  • Lifting a membrane then letting it go: /watch?v=NTYweevXd9I
  • Combining two liquids: /watch?v=dHqqwni02u4
  • Splashing of a 2D water drop: /watch?v=2l080jCpQhk
